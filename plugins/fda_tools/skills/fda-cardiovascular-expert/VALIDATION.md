### ✅ Clinical Endpoint Selection Guidance (17 MACE/TLR/TVR mentions, 24 VARC/MVARC/ARC mentions)
- **MACE definition:** Cardiac death + TV-MI + TLR (not all-cause mortality)
- **TLR vs TVR:** TLR preferred (stent ±5mm), TVR too broad
- **VARC-3:** Transcatheter valve endpoints (30-day/1-year composite, PVL, SVD)
- **MVARC:** Mitral valve endpoints (device success, heart failure hospitalization)
- **ARC-2:** Stent thrombosis classification (definite/probable/possible, acute/subacute/late/very late)

### ✅ Thrombogenicity Assessment Workflow (25 mentions)
- **ISO 10993-4 testing:** Platelet activation, coagulation cascade, complement activation
- **Ex vivo:** Chandler loop model (whole blood, 1-4 hours)
- **In vivo:** Large animal models (porcine/ovine, ≥90 days for permanent implants)
- **Device-specific:** Late stent thrombosis, leaflet thrombosis (TAVR), pump thrombosis (LVAD)
